# 📊 HR Analytics - Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges organizations face. High turnover increases recruitment costs, reduces productivity, and impacts overall business performance.

This project performs an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on an HR Analytics dataset using Python. The objective is to identify patterns behind employee attrition and provide actionable business insights that can help HR teams improve employee retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand employee demographics
- Analyze salary distribution
- Study employee attrition patterns
- Identify departments and job roles with high turnover
- Examine the relationship between employee satisfaction and attrition
- Explore correlations between numerical features
- Provide business recommendations for improving employee retention

## 📈 Key Insights

- Sales department recorded the highest employee attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians experienced the highest turnover among job roles.
- Employees in the lower salary slab showed significantly higher attrition.
- Overtime employees were more likely to leave the company.
- Job Level and Total Working Years showed strong positive correlation with Monthly Income.
- Employee satisfaction metrics contributed to understanding attrition trends.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve retention strategies for high-turnover departments.
- Review compensation for lower salary groups.
- Reduce excessive overtime where possible.
- Strengthen employee engagement and workplace satisfaction.
- Provide better career growth opportunities and promotion pathways.
